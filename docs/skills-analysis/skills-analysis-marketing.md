Copy de marketing para homepages, landing pages, pricing pages e outras páginas de conversão. Ênfase em especificidade, linguagem do cliente e mensagens orientadas a benefícios.

**Técnicas e Frameworks:**
- **Clareza sobre criatividade:** "If you have to choose between clear and creative, choose clear"
- **Benefits over Features:** Feature = o que faz. Benefit = o que significa para o cliente
- **Customer Language:** usar palavras exatas dos clientes (voice-of-customer de reviews, entrevistas, tickets)
- **Page Structure Framework:** Above the Fold (Headline + Subheadline + Primary CTA) + Core Sections (Social Proof, Problem/Pain, Solution/Benefits, How It Works, Objection Handling, Final CTA)
- **Headline Formulas:** "{Achieve outcome} without {pain point}", "The {category} for {audience}", "Never {unpleasant event} again"
- **CTA Formula:** [Action Verb] + [What They Get] + [Qualifier]
- **Writing Style Rules:** Simple > Complex, Specific > Vague, Active > Passive, Confident > Qualified, Show > Tell, Honest > Sensational
- **Quick Quality Check:** jargon, passive voice, exclamation points, buzzwords
- **Guidance por tipo de página:** Homepage, Landing Page, Pricing, Feature, About

**Agentes HAOS Beneficiados:**
